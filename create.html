How to Use

Creating Pieces

Click on a shape in the Palette to add it to the canvas, or use number keys 1-6.

Editing Pieces

  • Select: Click on a piece to select it. (Hold Ctrl to select multiple pieces or drag by holding left click)
  • Move: Drag a piece or use WASD
  • Rotate: R (Hold Shift to rotate counterclockwise)
  • Resize: E
  • Delete: T (Hold to delete all)
  • Undo: Ctrl+Z
  • Redo: Ctrl+Y or Ctrl+Shift+Z

Publishing

Once you have at least 3 pieces, click the button to publish. You can adjust the starting positions before confirming. You can place a maximum of 10 pieces.
